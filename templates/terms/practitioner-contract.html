This Telemedicine Platform Medical Practitioner Contract (the "Contract") is entered into between [ ResQ247 ] (referred to as "the Platform" or "the Company") and [ {{doctor.user.get_full_name}} ] (referred to as "the Practitioner"), collectively referred to as the "Parties," effective as of [ {% now 'd D, M Y' %} ].

Scope of Engagement

a. The Practitioner must be a registered and authorized medical practitioner in their area of practice.

b. The Practitioner agrees to offer a minimum of 12 hours and a maximum of 35 hours per week of telemedicine services on the Platform.

c. The Practitioner must adhere to the applicable code of ethics and professional standards of conduct.

Contract Term

The initial term of this Contract shall be one year, commencing on [ {% now 'd D, M Y' %} ]. This Contract is renewable upon mutual agreement of the Parties.

Terms and Conditions

The Practitioner agrees to the following terms and conditions:

a. Patient Interaction

i. The Practitioner will always maintain a professional and courteous demeanour towards patients.

ii. Patient feedback is essential and will be considered for future engagements.

b. Language Proficiency

The Practitioner must be fluent in both English and Swahili to effectively communicate with patients.

c. Non-Compete Clause

i. The Practitioner shall not solicit or draw away patients to their private clinics during or after the engagement with the Platform.

ii. The Practitioner shall not engage in any activities that may directly or indirectly compete with the Platform's services.

d. Communication Tools

The Practitioner agrees to use only the available tools provided by the Platform to communicate with patients and shall not offer alternative means of communication.

e. In-Person Consultations

Only authorized facilities, as determined by the Platform, will be allowed to conduct in-person consultations when necessary.

f. Referrals

If a referral is required, the Practitioner must refer the patient within the Platform's referral system. Consultation with the Platform's administrators is mandatory for any referrals outside the platform.

Disciplinary Measures

a. The Platform reserves the right to take disciplinary measures, including but not limited to warnings, temporary suspension, or termination of the Practitioner's contract, in the event of any contravention of the terms of this Contract, code of ethics, or professional standards.

Confidentiality and Privacy

The Practitioner shall adhere to strict patient confidentiality and privacy standards, ensuring the protection and confidentiality of all patient information in accordance with applicable laws and regulations.

Governing Law and Jurisdiction

This Contract shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of Kenya. Any disputes arising out of or in connection with this Contract shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts of Kenya.
