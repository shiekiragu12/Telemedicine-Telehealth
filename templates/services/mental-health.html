Welcome to our mental health telemedicine web platform. We understand that seeking help for mental health can be difficult, and we aim to provide a safe, accessible, and compassionate space for individuals to receive the care they need.

Our team of mental health professionals is committed to providing high-quality care through virtual appointments. We offer a range of services including therapy sessions, medication management, and assessments.

Resq247 is designed to be easy to use, secure, and private. You can access it from the comfort of your own home or wherever you feel most comfortable. Resq247 uses HIPAA-compliant encryption and secure video conferencing software to ensure your privacy is protected.

We believe that everyone deserves access to mental health care, regardless of their financial situation. That's why we offer affordable rates and accept most insurance plans. We also have a sliding scale fee option for those who need it.

Mental health is a complex issue that can affect every aspect of our lives. That's why we take a holistic approach to care. We understand that mental health is not just about treating symptoms but also about promoting overall well-being.

Resq247 offers resources and tools to support your mental health journey. We have a library of articles, videos, and podcasts covering a range of topics such as coping skills, self-care, and mental health education.

We also offer support groups and workshops led by licensed mental health professionals. These groups provide a safe and supportive environment to connect with others who are going through similar experiences.

At Resq247, we believe in the power of empathy and connection. Our mental health professionals are here to listen, support, and guide you through your mental health journey. We understand that seeking help can be challenging, but we are here to help you take the first step towards healing.
