What are Online Medical Referrals?

Online medical referrals are a digital way for patients to receive a referral from their primary care physician to a specialist. Instead of making an appointment with their doctor and then scheduling an appointment with a specialist, patients can now receive a referral online from this platform. Sign up and access healthcare at the comfort of your home.

Benefits of Online Medical Referrals

  • Convenience: Online medical referrals are more convenient for patients, as they can receive a referral from their primary doctor without having to schedule an in-person appointment.
  • Time-saving: Online referrals save time, as patients can receive their referral within minutes, instead of waiting days or even weeks to receive a referral from their doctor.
  • Increased Accessibility: Online referrals provide increased accessibility for patients, especially those in rural or remote areas who may not have access to a specialist.
  • Improved Coordination of Care: Online referrals improve coordination of care, as patients can receive a referral from their doctor and then schedule an appointment with a specialist, all in one place.

How Online Medical Referrals Work

  • Patients receive a referral request from their primary care physician through a patient portal.
  • The patient then selects a specialist from a list of specialists who are able to treat their condition.
  • The referral request is sent to the specialist, who then reviews the request and accepts or declines the referral.
  • If the referral is accepted, the patient can schedule an appointment with the specialist online.
