Technology has revolutionized the healthcare industry, We provide patients unmatched privilege access to medical care from the comfort of their own homes. In the field of laboratory services, telehealth has proven to be a valuable tool in providing patients with access to laboratory testing without the need for an in-person visit to the lab.

Our laboratories network across the country allows patients to receive laboratory testing through remote means such as video conferencing, phone calls, or instant messaging. Some of the services you can access :

  • Blood tests:We provide patients with access to blood tests, including complete blood count, blood glucose levels, cholesterol levels, and liver function tests, among many others. Sign up for free and find out more.
  • Urine tests:Telehealth laboratory services can provide patients with access to urine tests, including urinalysis and urine culture, among others.
  • Stool tests:Telehealth laboratory services can provide patients with access to stool tests, including fecal occult blood tests and stool culture, among others.
  • Infectious disease testing:we facilitate testing for infectious diseases, including COVID-19, influenza, and streptococcus, among others.

What are the advantages!

  • Convenience: Patients do not have to leave their homes or wait in long lines at the lab to receive laboratory testing.
  • Reduced risk of exposure: Telehealth laboratory services reduce the risk of exposure to infectious diseases, including COVID-19, by limiting in-person contact.
  • Cost-effective: Telehealth laboratory services are typically less expensive than in-person laboratory services.
  • Access to laboratory testing: Telehealth laboratory services provide patients with access to laboratory testing in areas with a shortage of laboratory services.
